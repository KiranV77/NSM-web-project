Objectives

  • To provide the students a platform to build and enrich their communicative competence.
  • To prepare the students to face situations with confidence and to seek employment in the related fields in the modern globalized world.
  • To enhance the level of literacy and asthetic experience and to develop creative and critical perspective.
  • To develop in the students an appreciation of nuances of literature and language through the understanding of the way English functions.
  • To provide students with the skills and knowledge necessary to work towards a research degree of their preference.

About the department

The department of languages has been growing under the illustrious shade of Naipunnya School of Management. Upgraded into a postgraduate department in 2014, department is served by 12 full time faculties and 3 guest faculties. Our hall of fame is adorned by university rank holders who have cleared UGC NET while undergoing 3rd and 4th semester of postgraduate programme. Department endorses a policy of communicative competence coupled with strict adherence to academic competence, and our curricular transaction is directed to this purpose.

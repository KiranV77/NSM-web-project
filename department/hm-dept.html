Courses Under Department of Hotel Management

BMS Hotel Management

Objectives

  • Academic: Examination by University / Board: To achieve 80% pass rate (individual subjects, with minimum C grade) in the University/ Board examinations.
  • Admission: 100% seats should be filled in within the stipulated time of admission for new batches.
  • Training (Industrial Visits):We will provide at least 15 hours training including refresh course to every faculty& students every year.
  • Placement:A minimum of 1 interview to be arranged for 100% of the eligible final year Hotel Management students. Industrial training arranged for all eligible final year students of Hotel Management (Fifth Semester ends)
  • Attendance:75% attendance has to be maintained by Students in each semester.

About the Department

The Department of Hotel Management was established in 2005 under Kerala University. The department has been upgraded as Naipunnya Institute of Hotel Management (NIHM). The department has been conceptualized to impart high quality and applied education at graduate levels to meet international hospitality standards and professional competence to students. On this backdrop Bachelor of Management Studies for Hotel Management programme is offered by the department. The programmes provide training from the management aspect of hotel industry with a focus on sustainable growth and development of the region. Our curriculum combines theory with authentic hands-on learning experience and students have numerous opportunities to interact with industry professionals. The Department currently has sanctioned posts as seven assistant professors, three lecturers (Instructors), one lab instructor and one training coordinator out of which the filled posts comprises of five assistant professors, three lecturers (Instructors), one lab instructor and one training/placement coordinator. The Department currently has a student’s intake of 216 and out of which all the seats are filled.
